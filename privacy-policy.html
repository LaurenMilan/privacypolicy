CourtCall PB

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

CourtCall PB ("we," "our," or "us") is a mobile app that helps pickleball players see who's at local courts in real time. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

Account information. When you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your email address and, if provided, your name. We use this only to identify your account — we never use it for marketing.

Profile information. You choose a skill rating (e.g., 3.5 — Intermediate). Your first name is optional and off by default.

Check-in data. When you check in at a court, we record which court, your skill rating, your chosen display name (if enabled), and your auto check-out time. This information is visible to other CourtCall PB users while you are checked in.

Location. We use your device's location to suggest nearby courts and to enable automatic check-in prompts when you arrive at a listed court. We do not store or share your precise location — it is used only on-device for proximity detection.

Usage data. We collect basic app analytics (screen views, errors) to improve the app. This data is aggregated and not tied to your identity.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To show live player counts and skill levels at courts
  • To let you check in and be visible to other players
  • To authenticate your account securely via Google or Apple
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising.

3. What Other Players Can See

When you are checked in at a court, other CourtCall PB users can see:

  • That someone with your skill rating is at that court
  • Your first name — only if you enable "Show my name" (off by default)
  • Your approximate remaining check-in time

Your email address is never visible to other users.

4. Third-Party Services

CourtCall PB uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — our backend database and authentication provider. Data is stored on Supabase's secure servers. See supabase.com/privacy.
  • Google Sign-In — for account authentication. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Sign in with Apple — for account authentication. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Retention

Check-in data is automatically cleared when you check out or when your auto check-out timer expires. Account information is retained until you delete your account. To request account deletion, contact us at the email below.

6. Children's Privacy

CourtCall PB is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We'll update the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
